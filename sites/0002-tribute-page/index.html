About Spaceman Spiff

Spaceman Spiff, interplanetary explorer extraordinaire, traverses the outermost reaches of the universe in a red flying saucer. Daringly dangling his dermis in the very dentistry of death, he triumphs over cruel and faraway climates, ferocious and disgusting aliens, and frequent engine trouble.
